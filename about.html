About Us

At Evergreen Enterprise Ltd, our journey began with a simple yet profound vision: to revolutionize the way businesses and individuals think about single-use packaging. Established in 1986, we have rapidly grown into a leading provider of eco-friendly to-go boxes, bags, and plastic utensils etc,serving a diverse clientele across various sectors.

Our Goal

Our primary goal is to reduce environmental impact while meeting the dynamic needs of various sectors. We focus on creating eco-friendly to-go boxes, bags, and plastic utensils that are both functional and environmentally conscious. Our commitment extends to continuously researching and developing materials and designs that minimize waste and promote recycling. We aim to lead the market in providing greener packaging options, catering to a diverse clientele who share our vision for a more sustainable future. Our mission is not just to sell products, but to inspire a shift towards responsible consumption and waste management in businesses and individual lifestyles.

Our Products

Our catalog includes biodegradable to-go boxes, compostable bags, and recyclable plastic utensils, all designed with sustainability in mind. Crafted from environmentally responsible materials, our products ensure reduced environmental impact without compromising quality or functionality. They cater to the evolving needs of diverse sectors, from food service to retail, emphasizing ease of use and durability. Our commitment to green innovation is evident in every item, reflecting our vision to revolutionize single-use packaging while supporting our customers' sustainability goals.
